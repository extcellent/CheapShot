📥 How to Add Downloaded Music to iTunes / Apple Music

  1. Open iTunes (or Apple Music app on Mac).
  2. Go to File → Add to Library (or drag & drop the MP3).
  3. Your music will appear in the "Recently Added" section.
  4. Right-click → Get Info to edit metadata (artist, album, cover art).
  5. Sync to your device via iCloud or USB.
