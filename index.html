🛡️ Safety Tutorial: How to Add Music to iTunes / Apple Music

▼
1

Open iTunes (or Apple Music app on Mac).

2

Go to File → Add to Library (or drag & drop the MP3 file into the app).

3

Your music will appear in the "Recently Added" section.

4

Right-click → Get Info to edit metadata (artist, album, cover art).

5

Sync to your device via iCloud or USB cable.

💡 Pro tip: Use Mp3tag to bulk-edit metadata before adding to iTunes.
